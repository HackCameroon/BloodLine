Project BloodLine

By Team Impulse

Whats all of this about?

Project Bloodline is a web application which provides a centralized platform for all blood donation operations within the country of Trinidad and Tobago.

According to the Ministry of Health Blood Transfusion Services within Trinidad and Tobago, an average of 20,000 units of blood are donated annually by the local population. This is well short of the recommended amount established by WHO which calculates to a required 65,000 units of blood per year. It is our duty to serve our country and people by creating software that can optimize the process of donating blood in order to incentivize and encourage those who are intimidated by the poorly structured process that is required to give blood. Utilizing the current technological platforms and technologies that are available to us, we can also be able to raise awareness and provide essential details for possible blood donors in a centralized and easy to use format. In doing so we can be able to directly impact the amount of blood being donated per year which can result in the saving of human life.

What can you do in Project BloodLine?

You can set up a profile with your medical information in order to automate the registration and test stages required when donating blood. This application contains the basic information and FAQs about blood donation, and places to donate blood all while in the comfort of your own home even if you're being forced to stay inside. The application also provides you with the capability to schedule appointments with your favourite blood donation centre and concurrently update their schedule on their preferred calendar software to ensure everything is prepped for your arrival.
